SaaSTools participates in affiliate programs. Who We Are

SaaSTools (saas.blogtrick.eu.org) provides free financial calculators and practical guides for bootstrapped SaaS founders. The site is operated independently and is not affiliated with any SaaS vendor.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. We do not use login systems, forms, or cookies that track individual users.

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how pages are visited in aggregate. Cloudflare's analytics is privacy-first: it does not use cookies, does not fingerprint individual visitors, and does not share data with advertisers. You can read Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.
